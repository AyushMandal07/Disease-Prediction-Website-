The Problem

Many people experience symptoms but struggle to get quick answers about their health concerns. It can be challenging to differentiate between various illnesses, especially when symptoms overlap. Our goal was to create a platform that makes diagnosing symptoms easier and more accessible.

The Solution

We created a web app where users can enter their symptoms, and the app uses a pre-trained Decision Tree Classifier model to predict the most likely disease. Here's how it works:

  • Symptom Input: Users input symptoms through a simple, user-friendly interface.
  • ML Model: A trained Decision Tree Classifier predicts diseases based on symptoms.
  • Instant Prediction: Users get quick predictions about potential health concerns.
  • Additional Info: The app provides disease descriptions, medications, and precautions.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy-to-use symptom input form for smooth user experience.
  • Accurate Predictions: Our machine learning model provides precise disease predictions.
  • Comprehensive Information: Detailed descriptions, precautions, and medications for diseases.
  • Educational Content: Informative blog posts on various health topics empower users.

Conclusion

Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App puts the power of machine learning and healthcare information at users' fingertips. By blending technology and healthcare, this app delivers fast, reliable, and accurate insights for health-conscious individuals. Our goal is to improve healthcare accessibility and empower users to take charge of their well-being.
